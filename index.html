Treasurebase is an online community for treasure enthusiasts. One-Eyed Willy's Hoard


Approximately around 1632, One-Eyed Willy had anchored his ship off the coast of what would later become the Goon Docks Area of Astoria, Oregon. Aboard his ship, The Inferno, One-Eyed Willy died with most of his treasure still aboard the ship. The treasure was looted in 1985 by a group of kids who named themselves The Goonies.


Suspected Location: Within Haystack Rock at Cannon Beach, Oregon


Wikipedia Page: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Goonies


Estimated Value: A single bag of gems aboard The Inferno sold for $1.74 Million in 1985.


Origin: Spanish/Portuguese
